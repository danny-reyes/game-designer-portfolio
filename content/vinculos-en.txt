Jon, a man caught between routine and hope, experiences a single day that will transform his life forever. An unexpected call propels him to run toward a destination charged with expectation and emotion. His journey, marked by urgency and longing, leads him to an experience as intimate as it is transcendental.

An intimate and visual story that explores, with subtlety, the deepest emotions of human connection. A brief but powerful story, where cinematic language merges with pure emotion, distinguished by its sensitive tone, poetic atmosphere, and ability to suggest profound feelings through minimal gestures.
