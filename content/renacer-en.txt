In an act charged with symbolism and emotion, a man faces fire, loss, and hope in a landscape that becomes a reflection of his soul. Rebirth is a visual poem about identity, memory, and the strength that persists even in the ashes.

A brief and symbolic piece that addresses themes of identity, belonging, migration, and the unbreakable bond with one's homeland. It stands out for its poetic tone and visual power to express the collective through the intimate.
